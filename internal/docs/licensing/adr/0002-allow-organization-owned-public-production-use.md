# Allow Organization-Owned Public Production Use

The canonical EULA will grant the licensee Organization the right to use React
on Rails Pro in its own applications, websites, and services, whether internal
or publicly accessible, because paid Production Use is intended to cover live
products used by end users. This corrects the contradictory phrase limiting the
grant to "internal applications" while preserving the prohibitions on
redistribution, sublicensing, providing the Software itself to third parties,
and using one Organization's license for a consulting client. The standard paid
grant will also omit the unqualified word `revocable`; paid licenses terminate
under the EULA's express fee, term, and termination provisions, while
Complimentary OSS licenses remain subject to the revocation terms in Section
4.1.

The approved Section 2 grant is:

> Subject to this Agreement, ShakaCode grants the subscribing Organization a
> non-exclusive, non-transferable license, during the applicable license term,
> to install, internally modify, and use React on Rails Pro in the
> Organization's own applications, websites, and services, whether accessed
> internally or by public end users.
>
> This grant does not permit redistribution or sublicensing of the Software,
> offering the Software itself to third parties, or using one Organization's
> license for another Organization or consulting client.
