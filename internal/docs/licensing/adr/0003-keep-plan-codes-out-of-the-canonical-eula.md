# Keep Operational Plan Codes Out of the Canonical EULA

The canonical EULA will define durable legal categories rather than giving the
runtime plan codes `partner`, `startup`, `nonprofit`, and `education` separate
legal meanings. Those codes remain internal entitlement or billing labels and
follow the EULA's standard paid Production Use, Complimentary OSS Production
Use, or royalty-free non-Production Use terms as applicable. Runtime attribution
policy will still require attribution for every recognized plan code.
